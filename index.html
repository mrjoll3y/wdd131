Firth, Idaho, USA

Firth, Idaho is a small rural town in southeastern Idaho, located near the Snake River and about 30 miles from Rexburg, home to BYU-Idaho. Known for its agricultural roots, Firth has a close-knit community and a peaceful, scenic environment with farmland and views of the nearby mountains. Firth is home to Firth High School, which serves as a hub for local activities and sports. Outdoor recreation is a big part of life in the area, with opportunities for fishing, hiking, and enjoying the natural beauty of the region.
